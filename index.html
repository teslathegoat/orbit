Cosmic Facts

Discover fascinating facts about our universe

Solar System

Our solar system is 4.6 billion years old and consists of the Sun and everything bound to it by gravity.

Meteor Showers

When Earth passes through the debris trail of a comet, we experience meteor showers with dozens of meteors per hour.

Exoplanets

There are more than 4,000 confirmed exoplanets in our galaxy, with billions more yet to be discovered.

Star Formation

Stars are born in nebulae when clouds of gas and dust collapse under their own gravity.

Space Exploration

Humans have been exploring space for over 60 years, with the first satellite launched in 1957.

Lunar Mysteries

The Moon is moving away from Earth at a rate of about 3.8 centimeters per year.
